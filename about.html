About Morox Properties

Est 2018

The Managing Director

Kagiso Tshepang Morokotso is a youthful Motswana entrepreneur, husband and realtor who hails from Mochudi. He is a real estate professional with extensive experience in Property Management, Property Valuations and Estate Agency. He has spent a significant period of his career working for PROMANCO in estate agency and valuations and has worked for Turnstar Holdings LTD as the Group Asset Manager.

Currently he is the Managing Director of MOROX Properties, a real estate company which is mandated in estate agency, property valuations and property management. He is also a part time lecturer at Baisago University in the Faculty of Built Environment. For the last year he has been teaching Diploma and Degree classes, as well as Certificate block release classes.
